About Nookly – Where Every Nook Feels Like Home

Nookly is a modern digital platform revolutionizing the way people discover and rent shared living spaces across India. Whether you're a student, a professional, or a family looking for a new home, Nookly simplifies the process of finding and managing PGs, flats, studio apartments, duplexes, and more.

Founded in 2023, Nookly is on a mission to transform the fragmented rental market into a seamless, user-friendly experience for all stakeholders.

Our platform is designed to empower both renters and property owners with the tools they need to connect, communicate, and collaborate effectively.

With a focus on verified listings, transparent communication, and a beautiful user interface, Nookly is redefining the rental experience in India.

Our Mission

To make housing discovery effortless and elegant for India’s urban renters by providing a secure, feature-rich platform that emphasizes quality, comfort, and clarity in every experience.

We aim to bridge the gap between renters and property owners, fostering trust and transparency in every interaction.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted digital rental platform, empowering renters and property owners through transparency, intelligent technology, and seamless communication.

Why Nookly?

The rental process in India is often fragmented and frustrating. Nookly addresses the key challenges faced by tenants and property owners:

  • Unverified or duplicate listings
  • Lack of transparency and digital communication
  • Dependence on brokers or middlemen
  • Outdated interfaces and poor user experience

Nookly solves these issues by creating a structured, technology-driven platform that prioritizes verified listings, real-time engagement, and a beautiful user experience.

Key Features

  • Wide range of rental categories: PGs, flats, studios, duplexes, and co-living spaces
  • Verified property listings with clear images and accurate details
  • Powerful filters: location, price, amenities, gender, food, distance from landmarks
  • Direct communication with property owners — no brokers involved
  • Digital rental agreements and documentation (upcoming)
  • AI-powered suggestions and smart alerts based on preferences

Who Nookly Serves

  • Students moving to a new city for education
  • Working professionals relocating for jobs
  • Families looking for affordable yet comfortable rentals
  • Remote workers and freelancers needing flexible spaces
  • Property owners and PG managers looking to manage listings efficiently

Core Values

  • Trust: Verified listings, secure communication
  • Empathy: Building with user needs in mind
  • Transparency: No hidden fees or misleading information
  • Innovation: Constant improvements through technology
  • Accessibility: Designed for everyone, everywhere

What’s Coming Next

  • Subscription tools for property owners
  • Rent payment integration

  • Expansion to multiple Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities
